Q: Will I get off the waitlist?

A: If you are not within the first 10 to 15 on the waitlist, it is unlikely that you will be given a spot. Due to the high demand, spaces fill up very quickly and priority will be given to those who are first on the waitlist, have majors that match the available hosts, and respond promptly to our invitation. We encourage you to look at other ROHP dates if your top choice is full.

Q: What should my parents do?

A: While ROHP is a program specifically for high school students, parents often accompany their children to Berkeley. If you and your parents arrive before ROHP begins, there are campus tours or classes that you can audit. A list of those classes can be found here. During ROHP, parents are encouraged to explore the city and campus. Unfortunately, we are unable to provide programming or housing for them.

Q: I’m not a Regents’ Scholarship candidate, can I still register?

A: Unfortunately, ROHP is available only to prospective scholars, as we are directly funded by the Regents’ and Chancellor’s Scholars Association and the Scholarship Office. If you are not a candidate, there are still many overnight programs put on by other student groups at UC Berkeley, such as OSP.

Q: How much does ROHP cost?

A: ROHP is free of charge to all of our attendees, and we work hard to keep it this way! We still do recommend that students bring a small amount of cash in case they want to purchase some souvenirs or try some local eats.

Q: Do I need to bring confirmation of my registration?

A: Yes! Please bring a printed out confirmation ticket from Eventbrite that has a barcode on it. We will be using that to check you in.

Q: Are all of the offered ROHP dates the same?

A: Yes, essentially. They will all follow a similar format and feature the same amount of activities. However, these will vary day by day, depending on speaker/performer availability. No program will be significantly different from another so you will not be missing out! Please check out the specific itinerary for each date if you are interested in the activities that day.

Q: Will my transportation (plane, car, train, etc.) to Berkeley be provided?

A: No, we are a completely student-run program and unfortunately cannot accommodate your transportation costs. If you are OUT OF STATE, you should have been contacted by Beth Berends or Carole Walker. You can find their contact information under the “Contact Us” tab.

Q: Is my space in ROHP guaranteed after I receive my confirmation email from Eventbrite?

A: No, you are required to submit two waivers electronically or faxed to us before we will officially match you with your host. If you do not do so before registration closes (1 week prior to the date), your space will be dropped and given to someone on the waitlist.

Q: I no longer want to/am able to attend ROHP but I have already registered on Eventbrite. What should I do?

A: You can cancel your registration following instructions found in your confirmation email from Eventbrite. Once you have done that, you will be dropped from our registration list.

Q: I really can't find the answer to my question on this website or the FAQs…

A: Email us at rohpberkeley2015@gmail.com. We may take a few business days to reply (we’re students too!)
