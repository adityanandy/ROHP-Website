What to bring to ROHP

Items You'll Need:

  • A fully charged phone and cell phone charger. You must bring a cell phone to ROHP and provide us with the phone number when you sign up. This is also how your hosts may contact you and vice versa.
  • Photo Identification (A school ID will suffice). This is the most important item.
  • A sleeping bag and pillow.
  • Toiletries and a towel.
  • Slippers for the showers.
  • Some money for your personal use (for breakfast the next day and, you know, to buy that Cal sweatshirt you've been coveting for years).
  • If you are having your scholarship interview, business or business-casual attire is highly recommended.

You will have a place to keep your luggage / clothes upon arrival at the venue. These items will be retrieved as your host comes to pick you up.
