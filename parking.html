Parking Information

NOTE: Do NOT leave your car in an area that is not meant for parking. Also, make sure the meter is paid for at all times, or else you put yourself at risk of receiving a hefty parking ticket.

At campus parking lots that do not have an attendant, visitors are required to pay in advance at a pay station. Always refer to posted lot and machine signs for parking rates and instructions.

RATES

Parking rates are approximately $1.75 to $2.00 PER HOUR. Please bring dollar bills (for machines) and quarters (for meters), if you plan on parking.

HOURS OF OPERATION

Monday-Sunday: 5:00 a.m. - 2:00 a.m. (Lots are closed from 2:00 a.m. - 5:00 a.m.). Hourly visitor parking is available in specified parking areas Monday-Friday from 5:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. After 5:00 p.m. on Monday - Friday and on weekends, hourly public parking is available in all campus parking areas unless otherwise indicated.

PARKING LOTS

TICKET DISPENSING MACHINES

Dispensing ticket machines accept $1 and $5 bills only. Please be sure to bring smaller bills.

    To operate a ticket machine:
  1. Press the yellow button.
  2. Lift the bill flap and insert bills (or coins).
  3. Press the green receipt button.
  4. Take the receipt and place it face up on the dashboard of your vehicle.

PUBLIC PARKING IN BERKELEY

Parking is cheaper and more available the farthur you park from campus. Sometimes, this parking is only a block or two away. There are a number of public parking lots and facilities near campus and in downtown Berkeley. This list includes municipal and privately owned parking lots and garages, which are open to the public. Please consult signs for hours and fees prior to entering.

  • Berkeley Way near Shattuck (2016 Berkeley Way)
  • Center Street near Shattuck (2025 Center Street)
  • Allston Way near Shattuck (2061 Allston Way)
  • Durant Avenue near Telegraph (2450 Durant Avenue)
  • Bancroft Way near Bowditch (2558 Bancroft Way)
  • Oxford Parking Garage (Oxford Street, between Allston and Kittredge)

There is street and metered parking near the University, but please be diligent about reading parking restrictions, as the regulations and schedules are STRICTLY enforced by the City of Berkeley.
