SCHEDULE FOR APRIL 3, 2015

Location: Hearst Memorial Mining Building.

Click for a view of the inside!

Planned Schedule (These are rough estimates):

    Day 1:

  • 5:00 PM - Guests check in at Hearst Memorial Mining Building lobby.
  • 5:15 PM - Mingling and group activities.
  • 6:00 PM - Formal opening remarks and keynote speaker: Lecturer Terry Johnson.
  • 6:15 PM - Dinner is served. Performances by ______INSERT HERE____ and _____INSERT HERE____.
  • 6:50 PM - Guests check out and take belongings to dorms.
  • 7:30 PM - Meet at Sproul Plaza for ROHP Night Tour.
  • 9:00 PM - Tour ends. Guests go to dorms or optional nighttime activities.

    • Day 2:

  • 9:00 AM - Guests meet for breakfast on campus.
  • 9:30 AM - Student panel.
  • 10:30 AM - Break into small groups for scavenger hunt.
  • 11:45 AM - Concluding remarks.
  • 12:00 PM - Program officially ends. Guests are encouraged to find lunch in Berkeley.
