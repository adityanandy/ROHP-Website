ROHP COMMITTEE

BEN JACKSON

Hello! My name is Ben Jackson, and I am excited to be one of ROHP’s Co-Coordinators for the upcoming school year. A third year at Berkeley, I’m enjoying double majoring in Molecular and Cell Biology and English with minors in music, sleep deprivation, and caffeine metabolism. In a typical day, you might find me studying lung cancer in my research lab, reading Greek tragedies in the library, or playing bassoon with the UC Berkeley Symphony Orchestra. Think my schedule sounds a little crazy? I’ll be honest: I have escaped from Alcatraz (I’m a swimmer too!) Before I graduate, my goal is to “borrow” the T-rex from the Valley Life Science Building as an attractive coat hanger for my dorm room. All kidding aside, I’m happy to talk to all of you about Berkeley, and look forward to seeing you at ROHP!
