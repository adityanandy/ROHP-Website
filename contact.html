CONTACTING THE ROHP COMMITTEE AND SCHOLARSHIP OFFICE

E-mail the ROHP Committee

The ROHP committee is responsible for putting together and running ROHP. If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to e-mail the committee at rohpberkeley2015@gmail.com.

E-mail Us »

E-mail Beth Berends

Beth Berends works at the scholarship office and is in charge of overseeing the Regents' Overnight Host Program. She can be reached at bberends@berkeley.edu.

E-mail Beth »

E-mail the ScholarConnect Team

The ScholarConnect team would be happy to answer any questions about contacting current Regents' Scholars at Cal that you can talk to. They can be reached at scholarconnect2015@gmail.com.

E-mail ScholarConnect »

How do I access the Facebook group?

To ask more general questions not found in our FAQ or to join a community of prospective students and scholar contacts, join our ScholarConnect Facebook group! All prospective students should have received an email invitation. For any questions about ScholarConnect or the ScholarConnect database, please email scholarconnect2015@gmail.com. If you believe that you should have received an access link to the prospective Regents' and Chancellor's Scholars' facebook group, please e-mail us at rohpberkeley2015@gmail.com.
