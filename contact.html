What is ScholarConnect?

ScholarConnect gives prospective Regents' Scholars an opportunity to reach out and connect to current scholars. Using the ScholarConnect database, you can find current Regents' Scholars by major, activities, etc. Feel free to contact any scholar you choose with any questions you have about Cal.

How do I access the Facebook group?

You should have received an access link to the prospective Regents' and Chancellor's Scholars' facebook group in an e-mail that we sent out. If you did not receive this e-mail or cannot access the group, please e-mail us at rohpberkeley2015@gmail.com. Only those who are invited for an interview are permitted to join this group.
