WHAT IS SCHOLARCONNECT?

Purpose of ScholarConnect

ScholarConnect is a new program that is being pioneered this year by RCSA. Its goal is to connect prospective Regents’ Scholars with current scholars at UC Berkeley. By looking through the ScholarConnect database, students can find scholars with similar majors, passions, and extracurricular activities. Students are encouraged to contact any scholar to find out more about their experience at Cal. There is also an FAQ for common questions that we receive. All our scholar contacts are volunteers who have chosen to devote their time and energy to answering your questions, so please don’t be shy!
