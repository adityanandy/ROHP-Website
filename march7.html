Schedule for March 7, 2015

Location: Hearst Memorial Mining Building.

Click for a view of the inside!

Planned Schedule (These are rough estimates):

  • 5:00 PM - Guests check in at Hearst Memorial Mining Building lobby.
  • 5:30 PM - Dinner is served. Faculty speaker: Professor Daniel Sargent and performances by Decadence A Capella and _______ INSERT HERE.
  • 6:00 PM - Student Panel.
  • 6:45 PM - Guests take belongings to dorms.
  • 7:30 PM - ROHP Night Tour.
  • 9:00 PM - Tour ends. Guests go to dorms or optional nighttime activities.
  • 8:00 AM - Program officially ends. Guests find breakfast independently or with hosts. Throughout the day, guests can continue to socialize with hosts or drop in on designated classes.
