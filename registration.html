REGISTRATION

The three forms that you NEED to fill out in order to attend ROHP.


  1. The Attendance Waiver
  2. The Medical Waiver
  3. Eventbrite

 Candidates for the Regents' and Chancellor's Scholarship who have been invited to interview with a faculty member for the scholarship qualify to participate in the RCSA Overnight Host Program (ROHP) put on by the Regents' and Chancellor's Scholars Association. ROHP is a free program.

 You must submit a signed Waiver of Liability (signed by your parents if you are under 18) AND a Medical Waiver to the Undergraduate Scholarships Office before getting confirmation that you can attend ROHP. The Waiver of Liability is here, and the Medical Waiver is here. Return the digital signature Waiver of Liability and Medical Waivers as an email attachment to uspho@berkeley.edu; or send by fax to (510) 666-2011.

 We need the Waiver of Liability AND Medical Waiver in order for you to attend ROHP. After the waivers have been submitted, you will be contacted one week prior to your ROHP date and will be matched with a host. You will NOT be paired with a host until both of the waivers are received. Contact uspho@berkeley.edu if you have questions about the ROHP Waiver of Liability or Medical Waiver. Waiver links are provided above.

 You must also submit a request to register through Eventbrite. You will select your preferred attendance date on the Eventbrite. We filled up fast last year, so it will be in your favor to sign up early!

Visit the Contact Us tab if you have any questions or concerns about the registration process. Go Bears!
